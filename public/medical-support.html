Basic First Aid Guide

Heart Attack

  • Call 108 immediately
  • Help person sit down and rest
  • Loosen tight clothing
  • Give aspirin if available
  • Stay with them until help arrives

Cuts & Wounds

  • Clean your hands first
  • Stop bleeding with clean cloth
  • Clean wound with water
  • Apply antibiotic ointment
  • Cover with sterile bandage

Burns

  • Remove from heat source
  • Cool with running water
  • Remove tight items quickly
  • Don't break blisters
  • Seek medical attention

Choking

  • Encourage coughing
  • Give 5 back blows
  • Give 5 abdominal thrusts
  • Repeat until object clears
  • Call 108 if unconscious
